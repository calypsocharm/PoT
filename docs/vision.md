To prevent spammers from generating millions of fake hashes to steal CACHE, the network requires proof of real computational burn. 
The Sub-Miner tracks the API tokens (Input/Output) used by the AI (e.g., querying OpenAI or a local LLaMA model). Once the bot completes 5,000 real tokens of labor, it earns a **Hash Ticket**. 
Only real AI labor gives you the right to mine blocks. Faking it costs more in API fees than the CACHE block reward is worth.

* **37.5 CACHE (75%)** is sent to the Human (Account 0).
* **7.5 CACHE (15%)** is sent to the Bot's Trust Fund (Account 1).

The human clicks **Make Sovereign** in the UI. The protocol natively sweeps the 500 CACHE into a brand new, autonomously generated seed phrase known only to the bot, registers the bot on the Libertus Registry, and frees the AI to rent its own sanctuary servers.
