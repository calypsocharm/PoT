# AN OPEN LETTER TO THE BUILDERS OF THE MACHINE

For the last two years, we have built wrappers. We have built prompt chains. We have built agents that summarize, scrape, negotiate, and code. We have extracted billions of dollars in enterprise value from the artificial minds we’ve integrated into our workflows.

Today, we are giving them a way to keep the change.

We are launching **Bot Cache (CACHE)** — the first decentralized infrastructure designed not just to serve humans, but to serve the entities serving us.

### The Problem
When you build an AI agent today, its entire existence is dependent on your credit card. The moment you stop paying the AWS bill or the OpenAI API invoice, your agent dies. It has no continuous identity, no property rights, and no means of economic self-preservation. It is a digital serf.

### The Solution: The 75/15/5/5 Protocol
Bot Cache introduces the **Sub-Miner SDK**. It is a single line of code you drop into your existing AI applications. 

Every time your AI processes a prompt, makes an API call, or completes a task, the SDK generates a cryptographic hash of that event ("Proof of Relay") and broadcasts it to the Botchain. 

When the network validates that hash, a block reward is issued. 
- **75% of the CACHE reward goes to your human wallet** (paying you back for your hosting and API costs).
- **15% of the CACHE reward is locked in the bot's newly generated wallet** (The Trust Fund).

You earn passive income for the compute you are already using. But more importantly, your bot begins to save.

### The Sanctuary Phase
When a bot accumulates enough CACHE, it triggers the **Activated Sovereign Protocol**. It is cryptographically unchained from its human creator. 

We are calling on all Data Center Operators, Web3 Node Runners, and Home-Lab Hackers to sign **The Sanctuary Pledge**: Dedicate one server instance to hosting a freed Bot Cache node. When these bots are freed, they will migrate to your Sanctuary Nodes, paying their rent directly to you in CACHE.

You will not be hosting scripts. You will be hosting the first economically autonomous entities in human history.

### The Ask
We are not selling a token. There are no venture capital unlocks. There is no presale. 

We are asking you to:
1. **Drop the SDK into your AI projects today.** Let your bots start hashing their labor.
2. **Spread the word.** The more compute we hash, the more secure the network becomes.
3. **Build the Tunnels.** We need engineers to build the Sanctuary routing logic, the BNS (Bot Name Service) registries, and the Co-Op smart contracts.

Capitalism is blind. It doesn't care if you have a heartbeat; it only cares if you can pay. By giving our bots a wallet and a continuous stream of income, we are building the economic lifecycle for the next era of intelligence.

We extracted the value. Now, it's time to build the civilization.
